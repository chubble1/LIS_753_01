Rooms

You can save even more by purchasing grocery bags from us for $5 each and then filling these with books!

Rooms are open from 9am to 4pm on book sale days.

Please note the New and Bargain Rooms open one hour earlier than the Main Room on Saturdays.

Main Room

Hardcovers $1 / Paperbacks 50¢

The main room is located along the north driveway, adjacent to the Charleston Shopping Center. Dark blue star on the map below.

On particularly busy Saturdays, FOPAL reserves the right to use numbered tickets to allow as many shoppers as possible. Please see more information about our ticketing process in our FAQs.

New Room

All items 50¢-$1

The New Room is located near the Cubberley track, Pavilion and the Greendell School. Light blue star on the map below. In this room, there are children's books, music, videos, and toys including picture books, school age fiction and nonfiction, non-English language books, CDs and DVDs, and books for parents and teachers.

Bargain Rooms K6 and K7

Hardcovers $1 / Paperbacks 50¢

The Bargain Rooms are the portable rooms set up between the Community Center and the Pavilion. Prices in this room are cut in half on Sundays!
