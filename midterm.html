Visit us at our monthly Friends of Palo Alto Library Book Sale!

We have over 50,000 wonderful used books, records, videos, magazines, and more. These are mostly donations from individuals, estates, and companies. We also sell items that the Palo Alto Library has removed from their shelves to make room for new purchases.

You'll find these same low prices on all the other tens of thousands of books that we have. While some booksellers may pledge a portion of your purchase to help local libraries, we give 100%. Plus, we charge no sales tax and we have the friendliest sales people you will ever find.

Our book sales occur the second weekend of each month all day Saturday and Sunday. The next book sale is scheduled for November 11th and 12th, 2023.

Visit our social media pages to stay up to date on book sales and how you can get involved!
